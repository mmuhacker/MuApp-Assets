Why does the Application ask for these permissions?

  • Microphone: This permission is used to record audio and convert it to written text (STT) to send it to the Artificial Intelligence model in the chat.
  • Notifications: To receive notifications in case of an update.
  • Access to Device Files: To enable the Application to save settings and keys so they can be easily retrieved when removing or clearing Application data, and to save documents, images, and long texts in the chat inside the package folder to facilitate the Application's access to them without using a large amount of LocalStorage, keeping the Application light and easy to use.
